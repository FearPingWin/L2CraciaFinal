You may raise the level of the clan. You will need the following:
Clan Level 1: 35,000 SP plus 650,000 adena
Clan Level 2: 150,000 SP plus 2,500,000 adena
Clan Level 3: 500,000 SP plus Blood Mark
Clan Level 4: 1,400,000 SP plus Alliance Manifesto
Clan Level 5: 3,500,000 SP plus Seal of Aspiration
Clan Level 6: 10,000 reputation score plus 30 or more clan members
Clan Level 7: 20, 000 reputation score plus 80 or more clan members
Clan Level 8: 40,000 reputation score plus 120 or more clan members
You may obtain the Blood Marks by hunting the bloody queen in Dragon Valley.
