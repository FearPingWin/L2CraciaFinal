To obtain the "Mark of Ketra's Alliance - Level 3," you must bring back 300 "Varka's Badges - Soldier," 200 "Varka's Badges - Captain," and 100 "Varka's Badges - General."
If you harm a Ketra Orc while carrying out this mission, your mark of ally will be downgraded one level and the alliance will be broken.

Monsters to Hunt - Varka Silenos Recruits, Varka Silenos Footmen, Varka Silenos Scouts, Varka Silenos Hunters, Varka Silenos Shamans, Varka Silenos Priests, Warriors, Mediums, Magus, Officers, Commanders, Elite Guards, Varka Silenos Great Magus, Generals, Great Seers, Varka's Prophets and Diciples of Prophet, Prophet Guards, Varka's Head Magus and Varka's Head Guards.
