To obtain the "Mark of Ketra's Alliance - Level 2," you must bring back 200 "Varka's Badges - Soldier" and 100 "Varka's Badges - Captain."
You can obtain them by killing: Varka Silenos Recruits, Varka Silenos Footmen, Varka Silenos Scouts, Varka Silenos Hunters, Varka Silenos Shamans, Varka Silenos Priests, Warriors, Mediums, Magus, Officers, Commanders and Elite Guards.
If you harm a Ketra Orc while carrying out this mission, your mark of ally will be downgraded one level and the alliance will be broken.
